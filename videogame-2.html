Video Game Module for Flipper Zero

Enhance your Flipper Zero with new gaming and development features.

Video Game Module

$49.00

The Video Game Module for Flipper Zero, powered by a Raspberry Pi RP2040 microcontroller, allows you to play games on your TV, use a built-in motion sensor for interactive experiences, and connect DIY components through a 14-pin GPIO port. It also features a USB-C port for direct PC communication and firmware updates, and can function as a standalone device.

  • Raspberry Pi RP2040 Microcontroller
  • Video Out for TV display
  • Motion Sensor with open API
  • 14-pin GPIO Port for DIY projects
  • USB-C Port for PC communication
  • Standalone Mode with alternative firmware
